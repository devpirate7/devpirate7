MOTION GRAPHS

Motion graphs provide an opportunity to analyze change in distance and time. Motion of an object can be indicated using DISTANCE , SPEED and ACCELERATION graphs.

When the vehicle is moving at constant speed, explore the DISTANCE , SPEED and ACCELERATION graphs.

When the vehicle is accelerating, explore the DISTANCE , SPEED and ACCELERATION graphs.
